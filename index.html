About

A designer who ships code, not just mockups.

I'm a BE Computer Science Engineering graduate who fell for interface design somewhere between debugging assignments and redesigning apps I used every day out of curiosity. That combination — engineering discipline plus a designer's eye — is still how I work.

Today I split my time between UI/UX design, frontend development, and digital marketing: researching a problem, prototyping the fix in Figma, then building it for real in HTML, CSS and JavaScript so it actually behaves the way it looked in the file.

I enjoy the unglamorous parts too — organizing messy data into dashboards, writing SQL to answer a question fast, or figuring out why a landing page isn't converting.
